Welcome

Welcome To The Chicken Bar


Welcome to our family-owned Chicken Bar, where our entire menu revolves around the delectable world of chicken. However, on Sundays, we switch things up and proudly serve a tantalizing array of mixed grilled meats.

Step into The Chicken Bar, where our mission is to elevate chicken cuisine to new heights. It's not merely about the bread, meat, sauces, and vegetables; it's the harmonious blend of these elements that promises a truly unique and delightful taste experience.

Immerse yourself in a cozy and inviting ambiance, perfect for sharing a pint with family and friends at any hour. Since our establishment in 1991, we've been dedicated to pampering our customers with exceptional chicken specials. To complement our already stellar menu, we now feature traditional 'grills and steaks' expertly cooked on a charcoal grill. Our chefs are tirelessly at work, ensuring that fresh and cold beer is always ready to accompany your visit, whether day or night.

Explore our extensive selection of ales and lagers hailing from the far reaches of the globe within our racks. Our amiable staff is here to guide you to the perfect choice, aiming to awaken and satisfy your appetite. We trust that your evening with us will be a thoroughly enjoyable experience.
